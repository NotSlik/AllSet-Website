About AllSet

A small, hungry crew with big standards.

Hey, I’m Laith Alawneh, owner and starter of AllSet. I’m a freshman studying business and I built AllSet to bring clean, reliable exterior and auto care to Iowa City without the big-company stiffness.

Our team is made up of local students and wrestlers from City High, independent and not affiliated with the school. We bring that wrestling-room work ethic to every driveway, storefront, and vehicle we touch.

We move fast without cutting corners, so you get sharp results without losing your whole day. We already clean and maintain a good portion of downtown Iowa City, and we’d be honored to add your home, cars, or business to the routes we take pride in.
